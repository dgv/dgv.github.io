recommended apps

Only open-source apps that respect your privacy, highly recommend to use Android-based alternatives like LineageOS on compatible devices or GrapheneOS for Google Pixel devices. For more information check surveillance self-defense.

browser (privacytests.org)

  • brave (source-code): the web3 (tor, ipfs and crypto support by default) browser.
  • duckduckgo (source-code): search engine browser (based on chromium) focused on privacy.
  • firefox focus (source-code): Firefox version focused on privacy and adblocking.
  • ironfox (source-code): fork of Divested Computing Group’s Mull Browser, based on Mozilla Firefox.
  • tor browser (source-code): onion routing browser.

connectivity

  • KDE connect (source-code): communication between all your devices, share files, phone notifications, remote control etc.
  • home assistant (source-code): companion app for home assistant automation that puts local control and privacy first.
  • orbot (source-code): tor vpn for smartphones.
  • protonvpn (source-code): vpn service from Proton.
  • tailscale (source-code): tailscale vpn service based on wireguard.
  • wireguard (source-code): modern vpn client.

email

  • protonmail (source-code): secure mail service with e2ee support from Proton (512MB of storage free).
  • tuta (source-code): secure mail service with e2ee support from Tutao (1GB of storage free).

maps & travel

  • organic maps (source-code): offline maps app for travelers, tourists, hikers, drivers and cyclists, privacy-focused fork of maps.me based on openstreetmap .
  • osmand (source-code): offline maps and navigation, also based on openstreetmap .

media

  • antennapod (source-code): foss podcast manager for android.
  • audiobookshelf (source-code): companion app for self-hosted audiobook and podcast server.
  • jellyfin (source-code, self-host): best open self-hosting media streaming solution.
  • vlc (source-code): best foss media player.

passwords & security

  • bitwarden (source-code, self-host): trusted pasword manager.
  • bitwarden authenticator (source-code): standalone app that generates and stores all your two-step verification tokens.
  • keepassdx (source-code): foss password safe.
  • proton pass (source-code): pasword manager from Proton.
  • yubico authenticator (source-code): manage two-step verification tokens from your yubico devices.

productivity

  • etar (source-code): foss calendar.
  • davx5 (source-code): sync your contacts, calendars and tasks.
  • proton calendar (source-code): calendar service from Proton.
  • tasks (source-code): foss to-do lists & reminders with sync support.
  • standard notes (source-code, self-host): sync notes, todos, spreadsheets and files with e2ee support.

social

  • element (source-code, self-host): federated secure communications, open alternative for Slack, Discord, etc.
  • mastodon (source-code, self-host): federated social network based on ActivityPub.
  • signal (source-code): best communication app with e2ee support.

storage & photos

  • ente (source-code, self-host): best alternative for Apple/Google Photos with e2ee support (10GB free).
  • immich (source-code, self-host): self-hosted photo and video management solution.
  • localsend (source-code): share files to nearby devices like airdrop but open and cross-platform.
  • nextcloud (source-code, self-host): easy and universal access to shared and/or personal files, your cloud self-host solution.
  • proton drive (source-code): storage solution from Proton (5GB free, must use rclone on linux).
